Common Questions

Quick answers before you contact us

How do I know if I won?

Winners are notified via the email address used to enter. Check your inbox (and spam folder) after the Sunday draw at 6:00 PM WAT.

I didn't receive a confirmation email

Check your spam or junk folder. If you still can't find it, try entering again with the same email address.

How do I receive my prize?

Winners are contacted via email with instructions to provide their Nigerian bank account details for direct transfer within 24-48 hours.

Can I enter from outside Nigeria?

The giveaway is currently only open to residents of Nigeria with a valid Nigerian bank account.

Protect Yourself from Scams

NaijaWins will NEVER ask you to:

  • Pay any fee to claim a prize
  • Share your bank login credentials or passwords
  • Send money to "release" your winnings
  • Click on suspicious links in emails or WhatsApp
  • Provide OTPs or PINs

If you receive any communication asking for these things, it is a scam. Please report it to us immediately at support@naijawins.digital.

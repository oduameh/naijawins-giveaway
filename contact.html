Common Questions

Before contacting us, check if your question is answered below:

How do I know if I won?

Winners are notified via the email address they used to enter. Check your inbox (and spam folder) after the Sunday draw at 6:00 PM WAT.

I didn't receive a confirmation email

Check your spam or junk folder. If you still can't find it, try entering again with the same email address.

How do I receive my prize?

Winners will be contacted via email with instructions to provide their Nigerian bank account details for direct transfer.

Can I enter from outside Nigeria?

The giveaway is currently only open to residents of Nigeria with a valid Nigerian bank account.

Important Notice

Please be aware that NaijaWins will never ask you to:

  • Pay any fee to claim a prize
  • Share your bank login credentials or passwords
  • Send money to "release" your winnings
  • Click on suspicious links in emails claiming to be from us

If you receive any communication asking for these things, it is likely a scam. Please report it to us immediately.

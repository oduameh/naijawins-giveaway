Education Savings Plans

Start saving early for children's education:

  • Target savings: Apps like Piggyvest, Cowrywise allow education goal-setting
  • Mutual funds: Invest in equity or balanced funds for long-term growth
  • Treasury bills: Low-risk option for shorter-term education savings
  • Education endowment policies: Insurance products combining savings and protection

How Much to Save

Calculate the total cost of education you're targeting, factor in inflation (8-15% annually for private education), and work backward to determine monthly savings needed.

Education Loans in Nigeria

Bank Education Loans

Many banks offer education financing:

  • Access Bank: Education finance for local and international schools
  • First Bank: School fees financing facility
  • Stanbic IBTC: Education loan products

Loan Features

  • Interest rates: 15-28% per annum
  • Tenure: Usually 1-5 years
  • Requirements: Salary account, employment confirmation
  • Disbursement: Often paid directly to school

Study Abroad Financing

International Student Loans

  • Prodigy Finance: No collateral, for specific programs/schools
  • MPower Financing: For US/Canada schools, no cosigner needed
  • Nigerian bank overseas loans: Usually require collateral

PTDF Scholarship

Petroleum Technology Development Fund offers scholarships for MSc and PhD abroad. Highly competitive but covers tuition and living expenses.

BEA/Agbami/Other Scholarships

Various corporate and government scholarships exist for undergraduate and postgraduate studies.

Scholarships and Grants

How to Find Scholarships

  • University websites (destination schools often have Nigerian-specific aid)
  • Scholarship databases (AfterSchoolAfrica, Scholars4Dev)
  • Corporate sponsors (MTN, Shell, Chevron, etc.)
  • Government programs (state and federal scholarships)

Application Tips

  • Start early‚Äîmost have early deadlines
  • Prepare strong essays and statements of purpose
  • Get recommendation letters early
  • Apply to multiple scholarships

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a loan without collateral for school fees?

Some banks offer unsecured education loans for employees with salary accounts. International lenders like Prodigy Finance and MPower don't require collateral for approved programs.

How early should I start saving for my child's education?

As early as possible‚Äîeven from birth. The power of compounding means early savings grow significantly over 15-20 years.

Are education loans worth it?

Depends on the education's return on investment. A degree that significantly increases earning potential may justify borrowing. Always calculate the total cost and compare to expected salary increases.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NaijaWins ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Email Address: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including pages visited, time spent, and referral sources.
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, IP address, and operating system for analytics and security.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us, we collect the information you provide in your message.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you our newsletter (if you subscribed)
  • Respond to your inquiries and messages
  • Improve our website and content
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Analyze website traffic (via Google Analytics)
  • Display relevant advertisements (via Google AdSense)
  • Remember your preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.

4. Advertising

We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect information, including:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics and traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense: Display advertising
  • Email Service Providers: Newsletter delivery

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties that help us operate our website
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@naijawins.digital.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@naijawins.digital.

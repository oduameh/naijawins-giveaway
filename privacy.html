Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how NaijaWins ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you visit our website and participate in our giveaway promotions.

1. Information We Collect

When you enter our giveaway, we collect:

  • Email Address: Required to enter the giveaway and contact winners.
  • Name: Optional, helps personalize communications.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, for analytics and fraud prevention.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time on site, for improving our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your giveaway entry and contact you if you win
  • Send promotional emails about future giveaways (if you opted in)
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure fair participation
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help operate our website.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger or sale of our business.

4. Advertising

We display advertisements through Google AdSense. These services may:

  • Use cookies to serve ads based on your interests
  • Collect information about your visits to this and other websites

You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to remember preferences, analyze traffic, and serve ads. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails

Contact us at privacy@naijawins.digital to exercise these rights.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, but no method is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

Our giveaway is not for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact Us

Questions? Email us at privacy@naijawins.digital

About NaijaWins

Nigeria's trusted weekly cash giveaway platform

Our Mission

At NaijaWins, our mission is simple: to give back to the Nigerian community by providing free opportunities to win cash prizes. We believe that everyone deserves a chance at financial relief, and we're committed to making that happen through our transparent, fair, and free weekly giveaways.

Since our founding, we've been dedicated to creating a platform where Nigerians can participate in legitimate giveaways without any hidden costs or catches. No purchase necessary, no hidden fees – just genuine opportunities to win real cash.

How NaijaWins Works

Our giveaway process is designed to be simple, transparent, and fair:

01

Free Registration

Participants enter by providing their email address. There's no purchase required and no payment is ever asked for.

02

Fair Selection

Winners are selected using certified random number generators to ensure every entry has an equal chance of winning.

03

Direct Payment

Winners receive their cash prize directly via bank transfer to their Nigerian bank account within 24-48 hours.

Our Commitment to Transparency

Transparency is at the core of everything we do. We understand that trust is earned, which is why we:

  • Announce winners publicly: Every winner is announced on our platform with their first name and city.
  • Provide clear terms: Our terms and conditions are written in plain language so everyone understands how the giveaway works.
  • Protect your data: We never sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Stay accessible: Our support team is available to answer any questions you may have about the giveaway process.

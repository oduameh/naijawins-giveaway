Frequently Asked Questions

Are these fintech apps safe?

Reputable fintech apps in Nigeria are regulated by CBN and/or SEC. However, no platform is 100% risk-free. Use established platforms with good track records and don't invest more than you can afford.

Which app has the highest savings interest?

Rates change frequently. Currently, fixed savings products (like Piggyvest SafeLock) tend to offer higher rates than regular savings accounts. Always check current rates before committing.

Can I use multiple fintech apps?

Yes, and it's often wise to do so. You might use one app for daily banking, another for savings, and another for investments. This also provides diversification.
